Friends

这是老友记的简介： The show follows three men and three women living in New York City, Monica, Chandler, Rachel, Phoebe, Joey, and Ross, who act as each other's best friends. The series premiered on September 22, 1994 on NBC-TV and ran for ten seasons, with the final episode premiering on May 6, 2004. The reunion special then went live on May 27, 2021 on streaming platform HBO Max.
